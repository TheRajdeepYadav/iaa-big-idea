Campaign summary Australia Amplified is a multicultural brand platform for Telstra that celebrates the diverse voices shaping modern Australia. The campaign uses community storytelling across digital, OOH, and social channels to create authentic connections with first-generation Australians and multicultural youth audiences. Key insight: 32% of Australians speak a language other than English at home — yet mainstream advertising rarely reflects this reality.
